User Experience Research

UX Research seeks to understand the behavior of users through site-testing and user-studies. I use an iterative design process depending on the area of research. I have experience with A/B tools such as Optimizely, VWO, and Google Optimize; I have also conducted user studies using UserZoom.

Software Engineering

Software Engineering is the systematic approach of designing, building, and testing software applications. I have experience with front-end development cycles, using HTML/CSS/JS (including JQuery and REACT), some PHP, and SQL, managed in a ticketing/workflow platform like JIRA.

User Interface Design

UI Design is the process of making interfaces in software with a focus on heuristics and aesthetics. Users should be able to intuitively understand how to use the product. I focus on structure, hierarchy, typography and colours. I have experience turning research insights into applicable features and designs.

Data and Marketing

Data Analytics and Marketing includes the process of generating and processing data to identify, anticipate, and satisfy customers’ needs. I have experience with data layer tagging and developing reporting architecture as well as implementing paid social media marketing through tools like GCP.
